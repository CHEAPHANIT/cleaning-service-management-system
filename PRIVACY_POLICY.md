# Privacy Policy for CleanNow

_Last updated: July 2026_

CleanNow is a university demo application for booking and managing home,
office, and apartment cleaning services. This policy explains what
information the app collects and how it is used.

## Information We Collect

- **Account information**: name, email address, phone number, and password
  (stored as provided during registration).
- **Booking information**: service selection, schedule, property details,
  address, extras, instructions, and payment method.
- **Cleaner information**: hourly rate, availability, assigned jobs, and
  completion documentation (photos and notes) for jobs performed.
- **Reviews**: ratings and comments submitted after a completed booking.
- **Sign-in information**: if you choose to sign in with Google or Facebook,
  we receive your name and email address from that provider to create or
  match your CleanNow account.

## How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely to operate the booking workflow:
matching customers with cleaners, tracking booking status, calculating
pricing, and displaying booking history, reviews, and notifications within
the app.

## Data Storage

Data is stored in a shared SQLite database operated for this project and,
on supported devices, a local on-device fallback database. No data is sold
or shared with third parties outside the services described above
(Google Sign-In, Facebook Login, and Firebase Authentication).

## Contact

This is a student/demo project. For questions about this policy, contact
the project maintainer via the GitHub repository:
https://github.com/CHEAPHANIT/cleaning-service-management-system
